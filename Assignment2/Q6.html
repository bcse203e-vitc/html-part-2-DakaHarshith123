Pasta with Tomato Basil Sauce

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and saut√© the minced garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the cherry tomatoes and cook until they start to break down.
  4. Season with salt and pepper. Toss in the cooked spaghetti.
  5. Mix well and garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving.

Nutrition Facts

  • Calories: 350
  • Protein: 12g
  • Carbohydrates: 50g
  • Fats: 10g
